SBS App Angular

Seedbytes-School is a web application designed to simplify the organization of an institution such as a school or school or gym. The objective is to facilitate communication between the different agents and to provide them with tools that simplify their daily tasks while facilitating the flow of information. My role was to create everything that is related to the teacher by offering him an ergonomic dashboard that assists him in his daily tasks. If you want to access you need to chose the -Enseignant- function, then you can use test@test.com as both username and password.
