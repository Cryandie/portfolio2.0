> Aestico Backoffice Nuxt3(VueJS)

I designed this Backoffice app for my company to provide employees with a centralized location for accessing customer information. The data is organized and grouped for easy access to the required information, and the app has real-time access to the database, ensuring that the information is always up-to-date.
I meticulously documented this project and kept a detailed record of all changes. As I juggle multiple tasks and projects within the company, this level of organization helps me stay on track and easily access the information I need.
